About KJSCE Hackathon
The KJSCE Hackathon 2016 saw the participation of all tech-savy people across Mumbai and we were in awe of the innovative ideas and brilliant products in display. The dedicated approach by the participants helped set a high standard for the competition in it's first year itself. Hackathon 2016 was held in kjsce as a part of the annual festival, Abhiyantriki. This tradition will be followed and we wil be hosting the Hackathon 2017 during Abhiyantriki 2018, a two day festival from 6th October to 7th October.
